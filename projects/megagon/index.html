Megagon
1,000,000 sides
This started off as a joke with a friend, but then became a reality. It's a very simple Python script which draws polygons using the Python Turtle module. But I pushed it to its limits and drew a megagon, taking just under 18hrs.
What's the point
It's a statement, more than anything. It's supposed to show how computers process things and how they take time and can be really pushed to their limits. I really wanted to see what software was capable of.
